Defining color value....!

Color in CSS must often specifies in the following formats:

  1. A COLOR Keyword - "red" , "green" , "blue" , "transparent" , ect.
  2. A HEX VALUE - like -"#ff0000" , "#00ff00" , etc
  3. A RGB VALUE - (RED ,GREEN, BLUE) - LIKE-"rgb(255 ,0 ,0)"
